✓ Key Points for VAs

  • Timing: Message 1 = immediate (5 min). Message 2 after they say text is ok. Message 3+ as soon as they reply. Don't wait more than 2-3 min between messages.
  • No response: Wait 3-5 min after Message 2. If no reply, call (double dial → voicemail → text "when's a good time for a quick call?")
  • If vague/unclear: Don't push for more text. Offer a call: "Easier to chat for 30 seconds?" Switch to phone immediately.
  • Booking language: Use day ranges not specific times. Always end with "what's the best number to reach you". Confirm exact time will be sent NEXT MORNING.
  • Soft closes (unqualified): Keep it short - don't convince. Use the "save our number" line. Wish them well.
  • Qualified closes: Recap the day. Set expectation about exact time tomorrow. Thank them. Short and warm.
